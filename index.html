About

I started BL Administrative Services to help support small- to medium-sized businesses with their administrative needs. I recognized that many employers have short-term or long-term project needs, but do not have the need for a full-time resource. Organizations need an expert who can come in, tackle a large brochure project, input crucial information into a database, assist with event planning, and jump in and provide program and project support. I worked 25+ years in the non-profit, education and for-profit sectors, focusing on process improvement and creating efficient work systems.
